20th anniversary of Michael Jordan's legendary shoe

In the Air Jordan series of shoes of the legendary Michael Jordan, there is a shoe model that stands out above all.

It was the last throw made by Michael Jordan for the Chicago Bulls in the final seconds of Game 6 of the 1998 NBA Finals series. It was the throw that gave Jordan Chicago a 1-point lead with 5.2 seconds left and then, he was crowned champion with his 6th ring before officially retiring.
On Michael Jordan's leg that day was a black, red-rimmed Air Jordan 14. This is simply a normal shoe model in the traditional black-red (often called Bred) color scheme of the Air Jordan shoe line.
