Display of 3,000 origami paper pigs

Domestic pigs or domestic pigs are a species domesticated from wild boars, bred to provide meat. Most domestic pigs have a thin undercoat on the surface of the skin.

Spain

INTERPORC, which represents all sectors of the pork supply chain in Spain, invited members in 19 cities across the country to create paper-folded pigs according to Japanese origami art. The event attracted more than 700 participants.

Total income

The group said it collected a total of 6,500 origami pigs from its cross-country tour, but only 3,000 were deemed of quality to qualify for the world record-setting effort.

Guinness World

Guinness World Records confirmed the event broke the record for the largest display of origami pigs.
