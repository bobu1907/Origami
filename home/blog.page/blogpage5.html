Design 3D models with paper

For centuries, architectural models have been playing an important role in the teaching and practice of architecture as well as being simulated in exhibitions and exams. 3D modeling allows architects and judges to explore the building through perspective, thereby understanding the structure of the construction system, and the relationship with the surrounding space.
In the process of implementing architectural or table models, the most important factor to create beautiful models is the material that makes the model. The materials used in the process of making architectural models or tools used in the process of making architectural models are quite diverse and paper is 1 of the options that are considered easy to shape to create vivid and realistic shimmering 3D models.
